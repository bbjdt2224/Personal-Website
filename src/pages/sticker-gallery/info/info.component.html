Placement

The sticker must be placed in an area as to not interfere with your phones antenna. Please use the below image to see where you can place your sticker on your phone

Using the Sticker

To use your sticker, hold it so the sticker is facing up. Next, have the other person hold the back of their phone to your sticker while their phone is on so that the sticker is near their phones reader. After a few seconds of it being in the right spot a notification will pop up with a link. The other person must follow that link to whatever you had programmed into your sticker.

iPhone

All iPhone X and later have an antenna.

The antenna for iPhones is located at the top of the phone

Android

Nearly all Android phones have an antenna, however, most phones come defaulted to not read NFC tags. If you wish to scan these stickers with your Android you must go to your connection settings and turn on NFC.

The Android antenna is located around the middle of the phone
